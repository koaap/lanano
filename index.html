Quark-based
breakfasts

Delicate texture, mild flavor, and natural ingredients make them an ideal choice for breakfast or a snack.

  • Zapekani

    Soft baked quark casserole with an almost cheesecake-like texture. A hearty breakfast or dessert you can top your way — berries, jam, condensed milk, or sour cream.

  • Quarnik

    Golden quark pancakes: lightly crisp outside, tender and creamy inside. Comfort-food flavor — perfect with tea, coffee, or as a warm breakfast.

  • Quarkini

    Quark doughnuts with a crispy golden shell and airy center. Lightly sweet and aromatic — delicious warm or chilled for an easy snack.

  • Quarkpie

    Quark pie with berries and oats — tasty, filling, and well-balanced. Perfect for a family breakfast, and great both warm and cold.

  • Kefir

    Thick kefir with a smooth, mildly tangy taste. A natural source of probiotics, protein, and calcium — great for daily balance. Enjoy it as a drink, with granola, or in baking.

  • Quarky (mini)

    Vanilla quarky mini-bar in chocolate glaze. The goodness of real quark with a delicate sweet taste — no milk-fat substitutes or artificial flavors. Great for school, work, or coffee breaks.

  • Sour cream

    Farm sour cream is made from raw farm milk. It’s perfect for topping syrniki and zapekanka, adding to soups and sauces, or serving with fruit and honey. A simple, traditional product with a pure dairy flavor.

  • Quarky (original)

    Vanilla quarky in chocolate glaze. The goodness of real quark with a delicate sweet taste — no milk-fat substitutes or artificial flavors. Great for school, work, or coffee breaks.

  • So4nik

    A childhood favorite: crumbly shortcrust pastry with a juicy quark filling. Perfect for breakfast or a quick snack — gentle, satisfying, and cozy.

  • Quarkeks

    Fluffy quark muffin — soft, lightly sweet, and very “family breakfast.” Great with coffee, tea, or milk, and stays tender longer thanks to the quark.

  • Tiramisoni

    Quark dessert with savoiardi, cocoa, and bright citrus notes. Chia seeds add a “superfood” touch — more texture, more goodness, longer-lasting satiety.

  • Quark (Cottage Cheese)

    Classic quark with a gentle milky taste and a light tang. No preservatives — just milk and cultures. Great on its own, with honey, fruit, herbs, or in baking.
